About DomiHive

Our Mission

DomiHive is a professional property management company that connects tenants with quality rental properties while handling all aspects of property management for landlords. We eliminate the stress and uncertainty from the rental process by providing verified listings, secure payments, and professional management services.

What We Do

We manage rental properties on behalf of landlords, handling tenant screening, rent collection, maintenance coordination, and property marketing. For tenants, we provide a curated selection of verified properties with a streamlined application process and professional support throughout their tenancy.

Our Values

  • Transparency: No hidden fees or surprises
  • Professionalism: Managed by experienced property experts
  • Security: Escrow-protected payments and verified listings
  • Convenience: Streamlined processes from application to move-in
  • Support: Dedicated customer service for tenants and landlords
